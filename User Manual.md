# Ventilated Façade Energy Feasibility 🏢⚡

Welcome! This is application to evaluate the summer cooling energy demand for buildings equipped with a ventilated façade. This tool processes high-resolution CFD results from a pre-calculated numerical database.

In this document is explained how to launch the software and organize the files to ensure everything works correctly.



## 🚀 How to Launch the Software

1. Double-click on the `Ventilated Facade EF.exe` file.
2. Wait a few seconds for the graphical interface to open.



## 🛠️ Basic Usage

1. **Select the city** from the first dropdown menu.
2. **Set the desired parameters** (Building height $H_b$, Cavity depth $D_c$, Thermal Transmittance $U$, Façade's Solar absorptance and infrared emissivity $\alpha_{sol}$ - $\varepsilon_{inf}$ properties, façade's orientation, and month).
3. **Calculate:** Click on the blue "Calculate Cooling Energy" button.
4. **Results:** Read the output energy parameters for the building with or without a ventilated façade at the bottom of the interface.
